Moving files between computers

Sharing the folder on the network

  1. Open File Explorer on your computer
  2. Go to doccuments
  3. Right click in the open space and click on New then Folder
  4. Name the folder and then click enter
  5. Right click on the folder and then click Properties
  6. On the properties window click the Sharing tab
  7. Then click the Advanced Sharing... button
  8. Tick the box that says Share this folder
  9. Click ok
  10. Click ok again
  11. Now your folder is shared with everyone on your network

Using a USB stick

  1. Insert USB stick into the computer
  2. Open two File Explorer windows on your computer
  3. In one widow click on your USB stick to open it
  4. In the next window go to the file you want to move
  5. Drag the file onto the USB stick window
  6. Wait for it to copy
  7. On the left side on the window right click the USB stick and click eject
  8. Remove your USB stick from the computer
