Connecting to the Internet

Setting Up The Modem

  1. Plug the modem ehternet cable into your wireless router
  2. Plug the modem into the wall socket
  3. Plug in the modems power cord
  4. Wait for the modem to boot

Setting up the wireless router

  1. Plug router into the wall
  2. Look on the back of the router for network name and password
  3. Wait for the router to boot

Connecting to the wireless router

  1. On your computer go to settings
  2. Click Network and Internet
  3. Click on wifi
  4. Find your network name and click on it
  5. Type in your password and click connect
