How to Install AVG AntiVirus

Download and Install AVG

  1. Click the name of your product to download the installation file:
    • AVG AntiVirus
    • AVG Internet Security
  2. Run the downloaded file to start the installation process.
  3. Follow the instructions on your screen.
  4. When prompted, enter your AVG license number. The license number is sent automatically to your e-mail once purchased.
  5. Complete the installation and restart your computer.

Activate AVG

  1. Open AVG (double-click the AVG icon on your Desktop, or the AVG icon in the notification area in the bottom-right corner of your computer screen).
  2. In the AVG window click Options, and then click Activate or Reactivate.
  3. Insert the License Number that you received in an email after purchasing AVG into the New license number field, and then click Activate.
  4. Your AVG will now be activated with a new license number. You can verify this by clicking Support in the top menu. Your license number and validity will be shown, along with other product information.
